AARHUS CITYSEC

CitySec is an unaffiliated collection of informal meetups for like-minded security professionals.

We have booked the wooden terace with room for 60 people at "Nicolinehus"(Aarhus food marked), where we will start at 16.00 with beers/drinks/snacks and eat dinner whenever it fits you.

Registration is no longer required to attend citysec events in Aarhus. You just show up, talk, eat and have fun with like-minded people.

Where: Nicoline Koch Plads 31, 8000 Aarhus
